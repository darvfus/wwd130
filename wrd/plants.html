Chamomile (Matricaria chamomilla):

Chamomile is used to relieve digestive issues and as a natural relaxant. You can prepare an infusion with dried chamomile flowers and hot water. Let it steep for a few minutes and then drink.

Mint (Mentha):

Mint is used to alleviate digestive issues and as a relaxant. Prepare an infusion with dried mint leaves and hot water.

Aloe vera (Aloe barbadensis)

Aloe vera gel is used topically to treat burns and skin issues. Simply apply the gel directly to the affected skin.

Ginseng (Panax ginseng) :

Ginseng is used to increase energy and endurance. You can consume it as tea or in capsule form following product instructions.

Cúrcuma (Curcuma longa) :

La cúrcuma es conocida por sus propiedades antiinflamatorias. Puedes añadirla como condimento a tus comidas o preparar una bebida de leche dorada con leche y cúrcuma.
